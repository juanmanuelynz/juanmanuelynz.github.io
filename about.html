About me

First of all I can say that I am a Human, and I know it sounds weird or funny to clarify, but over the years I've seen people label and define themselves just by their profession or what they do. Sure I'm not an evolved cat or a talking eggplant, but before anything else, I'm a Human with a capital H. I believe that being a Human demands self-discovery as the number one priority in life, a path that makes us aware of our light as well as our darkness.

I am a transparent being, with honest listening, lover of life. Happy by nature, curious and hungry for all kinds of knowledge. I find inspiration in the vulnerability of the Being, in philosophy and theosophy, in sacred geometry and anthropological symbolisms. Along with gardening, my number one hobby is illustration. I dream with telling a story, a story about each one of us being the best version of ourselves. A story that could help others (and help me) to awake the sleeping seeds within us, that makes roots grow in our soul and be reborn from there, with our true essence and voice.

Now, professionally speaking, I started my career as a developer. Initially Back-end but quickly finding in the Front-end the possibility of creating visual impact. That search led me to delve into the audiovisual world and Product Design. Thanks to this broad perspective, I feed my intuition and search for beauty with mathematics and logic, I am detail-oriented with a pixel vision. I have worked developing management systems, video games and web applications. I have designed products for social and financial enterprises. I co-founded a startup, Cinemad.tv, where I designed and developed from scratch a platform to generate and edit interactive videos. In this period I had the chance to participate and succeed in Start-Up Chile program and its extention Scale-Up, and be part of Seedstars World community in Switzerland.

After +15 years, I have found a new turn in my path, another point of view to continue expanding my gaze. A challenge that has taken me from Buenos Aires to Tallinn, to join as a mentor at codesters.club, with the purpose of leading youngsters in secondary schools through the process of ideating, designing and developing digital products.
